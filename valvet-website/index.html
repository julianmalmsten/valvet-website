ABOUT

Valvet is an alternative rock band from Sweden blending cinematic soundscapes with raw, emotional energy. Formed by four friends with a shared love for storytelling through music, Valvet’s songs capture the highs and lows of the human experience.

With influences ranging from indie rock to post-rock, Valvet creates music that resonates with both heart and mind. Join us on our journey — from small clubs to big stages.
